Join the Team


​If you are a top notch athlete looking for a highly competitive team in the DC area, we hope you will contact GRC. Though our standards for new members are high, we are always looking for promising new talent.

To indicate your interest in GRC, send us an email at info@grcrunning.com. Please be sure to include the following in your initial email:
  • Your recent personal best times
  • Your running history (describe your progression as a runner including personal best times each year starting in high school, if applicable)
  • Injury history
  • Your current level of fitness
  • Racing goals for the coming months.
