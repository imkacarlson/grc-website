Georgetown Running Club

Matt Hassett, PhD

Matt, who hails from North Bellmore, NY, started running in his freshman year at Chaminade High School. He continued his running career at Loyola University in Baltimore, where he was a 2 time cross country co-captain and holds top 10 times in school history in the 8K and 10k. Matt joined GRC in August 2012 after moving to Washington. He received his Ph.D in Chemistry from Georgetown University in April 2017.
