Georgetown Running Club

Keith Carlson

Keith was introduced to running when his parents started training for the 2007 San Francisco Marathon, and he biked alongside them. After Keith beat his Dad in a 2 mile race, he was hooked. Keith ran four years of cross country in high school, and continued his career at Willamette University. During his time at Willamette, Keith ran in four Division III national meets, contributed to three conference team titles, and is 4th on Willamette's all time 5000 list. Keith's goal with GRC is to keep the momentum going and run more PRs. Keith is a graduate student at George Washington University focusing on Cybersecurity in Computer Science.
