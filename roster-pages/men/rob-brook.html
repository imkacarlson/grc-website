Georgetown Running Club

Rob Brook

Inspired by his beloved Patriots, Rob grew up playing football despite extremely limited hand-eye coordination. At his freshman football coach's recommendation, he tried indoor track and became a decidedly below average 55/110m meter hurdler. After realizing there wasn't much of a future in football Rob focused on track. In Rob's inaugural season of distance running, he qualified for the All-State Meet in XC as an individual.
