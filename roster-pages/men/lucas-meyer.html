Lucas Meyer

Luke had a stellar career at Yale, where he won multiple Heps championships, and was an All-American in cross country and outdoor track. He competed in the 2008 Olympic Trials in the steeplechase, and the 2012 Olympic Trials in the marathon. Luke's goals include improving his marathon PR, and as a reach, making the A team for GRC at the USATF Club Cross Country Championship. Luke works as an attorney in private practice in Washington.
