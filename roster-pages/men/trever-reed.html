Georgetown Running Club

Trever Reed

Trever started running as a sophomore in high school mainly to stay in shape for varsity basketball. After 4 years of trying to juggle basketball and distance running, he made the switch to running full time in the middle of his sophomore year at Miscercorida University, after spending most of his freshman basketball season on the bench. During his college career, Trever placed 4th in the conference in the 10,000, and 13th in the conference in cross country, and was a 2 year captain, and a 2 time coaches' award winner.

Trever currently works as an outpatient orthopedic physical therapist for Pivot Physical Therapy.
