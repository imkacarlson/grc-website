Alex studied mechanical engineering at Johns Hopkins University, where he qualified for the 2019 NCAA D-III indoor championships in the 3000 and set the school record in that event for 2 years, and competed in two NCAA XC championships. Alex's goals with GRC are to improve his PRs, and try some events that he did not run in college. Alex works as strategy consulting analyst Dean & Company in Northern Virginia.
