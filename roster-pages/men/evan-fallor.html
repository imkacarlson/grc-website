Georgetown Running Club

Evan Fallor

Evan began running in sophomore year of high school when his hopes and dreams of making it as an NBA sharpshooter were dashed by his sub-6 foot height. Though he enjoyed moderate success in the sport, he took several years off from running in college before rediscovering it in 2015. Prior to joining GRC, Evan spent five years running for Coach Jerry with Northern Virginia Running Club, where he set PRs across every distance. A traumatic brain injury Evan suffered while running in November 2019 has ironically provided him a greater appreciation for the sport, and he hopes to tap into his potential training with GRC. Evan hopes to break 2:30 in the marathon as a member of GRC while gaining some PRs in the shorter distances along the way. By day, Evan is a financial journalist and master's student at Johns Hopkins University.
