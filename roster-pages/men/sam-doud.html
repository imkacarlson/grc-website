Sam Doud

The self-proclaimed Terror of the Towpath, “Mileage” Sam lives off the tears of his outsoles as he grinds them into the ground (and a healthy bowl or two of oats). By day he taps away at his keyboard as a software developer at GEICO and dreams of Capital Crescent Trail.

Taking a circuitous path through collegiate running, Sam found himself 20 miles into a marathon and realized that was his calling. He hopes to knock at least a minute off his PR and quality for the 2020 Olympic Trials and run a sub-65 400m this year.
