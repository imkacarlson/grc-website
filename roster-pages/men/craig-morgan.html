Georgetown Running Club

Craig Morgan

Craig started running his freshman year of high school to stay in shape for lacrosse, and by the time his high school career was over Craig won 10 state titles in Maryland representing River Hill High School. Craig went on to run for the University of Maryland, and used his final year of eligibility as a graduate student at George Mason. Craig is 4th on the Maryland all-time list in the 1000 meters, and while at Mason he was the Atlantic 10 conference champion in the 1000. Craig's goals with GRC are to improve on his PR's and to start dabbling in some longer races. Craig works in business-to-business sales for a security and compliance company.
