Georgetown Running Club

Pat Kuhlmann

Pat returned to competitive running a decade and a half after finishing his college career. Pat has focused on the marathon, placing 3rd in the Masters Division at the 2016 Chicago Marathon, 2nd in the Masters Division at the 2015 Chicago Marathon, 3rd in the Masters Division at the 2015 Houston Marathon, 7th in the Masters Division at the 2014 Boston Marathon, 2nd in the Masters Division at the 2012 California International Marathon, and 5th in the Masters Division at the 2011 Boston Marathon (31st overall), winning the Masters Division at the 2011 California International Marathon, and running a personal best of 2:28:01 at the 2013 Boston Marathon. Pat hopes to improve his marathon personal best, however slightly, and set a few Masters personal bests at shorter distances. Pat works as an attorney in the Legal Policy Section of the Antitrust Division of the U.S. Department of Justice.
