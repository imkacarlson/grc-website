Georgetown Running Club

Michael Crozier

Croz began running as a freshman at Gonzaga College High School in DC in the fall of 2008, and he immediately enjoyed all of his daily sojourns to the National Mall, Arboretum, and polo fields. Croz became Gonzaga's first cross-country All-American in 2011. He ran track and cross country at Georgetown University, where he was an All-American in the 10,000 in his final collegiate race in June of 2018. Croz studied Public Policy at Georgetown's McCourt School, and is currently an analyst at LMI.
