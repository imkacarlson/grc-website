Georgetown Running Club

Mitch Welter

Mitch picked up running in high school after quickly learning that sports requiring coordination were not for him. In college, he ran for University of Maryland, College Park’s club team where he really fell in love with the sport. Now with GRC, Mitch hopes to improve on his 3000m and 5000m PRs and eventually move up to longer road races. Professionally Mitch is pursuing a career in social work and is currently working with children at a homeless shelter in Prince George’s County, Maryland.
