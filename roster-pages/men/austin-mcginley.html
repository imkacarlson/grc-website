Austin McGinley

Austin began running in high school to get in shape for soccer, and he has been running since. Austin was the Maryland 2A state cross country champion in 2011, and during his college career at Shippensburg he was a Division II All-American in cross country. With GRC, Austin expects to transition to the marathon, and he intends to run some PRs at shorter distances along the way. Austin words as a GIS Specialist for LMI in Arlington.
